# Data Quality Turnaround Case Study

## Context
Frequent data issues eroding stakeholder trust.

## Approach
- Implemented a Python-based DQ framework with Great Expectations and pytest.
- Integrated checks into CI/CD and nightly pipelines.
- Defined clear ownership and SLAs with domain teams.

## Impact
- 60% reduction in data quality incidents.
- 99.5% metric accuracy for key dashboards.
- Created a culture of "truth-seeking" and transparency around data.
