# AI Governance Enablement Case Study

## Context
Interest in GPT/LLM solutions without clear governance or risk framework.

## Approach
- Built governed pipelines for chatbots, document translation, and summarization.
- Introduced policy tags, model allow-lists, and observability.
- Collaborated with legal, security, and product on usage guidelines.

## Impact
- Safely launched 3+ AI use cases with measurable productivity gains.
- Reduced manual analysis effort for certain workflows.
- Positioned the team as go-to experts for responsible AI.
